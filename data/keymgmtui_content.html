About Keys

When you hide text, that text is replaced with phrases taken from your key. Similarly, when you unhide text, this Hide in Plain Sight add-on reconstructs the original text from the encoded text.

The key must be shared between all the parties you intend to share that message. A single key may be re-used for multiple messages. However, you must keep your keys secret. If the key is discovered by the bad guys, they can decode your previous messages.

Keys are generated from a source text. The source text should be moderate in size: try something a couple of chapters in length. You specify the key by providing a URL to its location. The file is downloaded only once during generation and the computed key value is then stored on your computer. Make sure the page does not change between you and your other parties key generation! You can reference a local file using a file: URL.
